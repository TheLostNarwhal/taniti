LODGING

  • Sea Breeze Hotel   |   4.9/5
    Perfectly situated between convenience and relaxation, this resort has a lot to offer, with ocean views for every room, yet a mere-minutes walk from the city. Guests can expect quality services, including a hotel shuttle, valet, and room service.
    Book now!
  • Chief Kolom Resort   |   4.4/5
    Experience the sea like never before! This resort is literally on the Yellow Leaf Bay! Live in the style of the native islanders, but without having to sacrifice any of the modern comforts. (Jet-skis, paddle boards, and kayaks available upon request.)
    Book now!
  • Betsy's Bed & Breakfast   |   3.8/5
    A tropical vacation doesn't mean you have to live at a resort! Visit Betsy's Bed and Breakfast for that familiar "at-home" environment. Smell fresh pancakes and fruit juice every morning, and join us for the nightly bonfire and traditional islander songs every evening.
    Book now!
  • Beach House #117   |   3.2/5
    Standard beach house for 4 guests. Located near Pineapple Beach, this beach house puts you in the middle of all the fun activities! Experience wild animal sightings, weekily sport tournaments, and frequent events right from your living room window!
    Book now!
